Training Register

Index of all Cethos trainings — interactive in-portal modules and controlled staff guides

Version 1.0  ·  26 June 2026  ·  Doc REG-TRN-001

This register lists every active Cethos training. Trainings provide the competence and training-record evidence required by ISO 17100 (competence & CPD) and the IQVIA audit. Cethos delivers training in two formats:

  • Interactive in-portal modules — lesson-by-lesson, assignable, with completion (and where enabled, a quiz) recorded per person.
  • Controlled staff guides — versioned documents in Documents & Manuals, read and evidenced via the training record.
Completion = evidence. For interactive modules, each learner acknowledges every lesson (and passes the quiz where required); completions are the auditable training file. For guides, completion is recorded on FORM-TR-001 (Staff Training & Competence Record).
