## 3. COA Qualification Pathway (SOP-019)

All five gates must be met before a linguist is assigned to any COA service:

| Gate | Requirement | Where to see it in the portal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Role qualification — ISO §3.1.4 basis, Tier-2 verified evidence | Vendor → QUALITY → Qualifications |
| 2 | Subject-matter qualification — Life Sciences / Clinical | Vendor → QUALITY → Subject Matter |
| 3 | Active NDA | Vendor → QUALITY → NDA Status |
| 4 | COA LV training completed and recorded | Vendor → QUALITY → Training |
| 5 | Competence: ≥90% quiz pass + graded sample (MQM-scored) | Vendor → QUALITY → Competence Evidence |

**Assignment gate:** The portal blocks assignment of any non-COA-qualified vendor to COA workflow services (cognitive debriefing, clinician review, reconciliation, harmonization, back-translation, LV migration, screenshot review, post-review).

**COA LV Status panel:** Every vendor profile's QUALITY tab shows a green/amber/grey COA LV status panel that summarises qualification at a glance.
