## 1. Introduction

This document provides a comprehensive list of all Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) that comprise the Quality Management System at Cethos Translation Services. These SOPs provide detailed instructions for conducting all critical processes and ensure consistent, high-quality service delivery to our pharmaceutical and medical device industry clients.

All SOPs are controlled documents subject to the document control procedures outlined in SOP-001. Each SOP is reviewed at least annually and updated as necessary to reflect current best practices, regulatory requirements, and lessons learned.

This list contains the **15 core SOPs** that govern translation, clinical, and quality processes (SOP-001 to SOP-015), together with **6 extension SOPs** (SOP-016 to SOP-021) covering information-technology, business-continuity, and COA linguistic-validation processes — **21 SOPs** in total. Version 6.0 reconciles the operational SOP set maintained in the Cethos portal with this register so that document numbering is consistent across the QMS.

**Status key:** *Active* = approved and in force · *Draft* = authored, pending approval · *Registered* = topic owned, full procedure document in preparation.
