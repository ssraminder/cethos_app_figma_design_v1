## How to use this worksheet
**Purpose:** confirm each SOP is **accurate to how Cethos actually works** and **free of obvious errors.** You are the reality check — you are **not** judging ISO or legal wording (that is Raminder's job).

**Golden rule — do not change anything.** If something is wrong, write it under "Issues"; Raminder will fix it.

**To open the SOPs:** log in to **portal.cethos.com**, go to **/admin/sops**. Each SOP opens and shows its full text (there is a Word-export button if you prefer to read offline).

**Work through one SOP at a time, in the order below.** For each, run the 7 checks, tick the ones that are fine, and note any problem.

## The 7 checks
1. **Opens** — the SOP displays its full text (not blank, no error).
2. **Number matches** — the SOP number in the title is the same number shown in the heading inside the document.
3. **Signatures correct** — the approval block reads **Prepared: Raminder Shah (Acting Quality Manager) · Reviewed: Amrita Shah (Managing Director) · Approved: Raminder Shah (Founder & CEO)**. Flag immediately if you see **"Maria Teressa"** or **"Usman Khan."**
4. **Accurate** — it describes what we actually do; nothing wrong or invented.
5. **No blanks** — no "[fill in]", "[TBD]", "TODO", or unfinished sentences.
6. **Cross-references exist** — if it says "see SOP-0XX", that SOP is in the list.
7. **Date / version** — effective date June 24, 2026; version 1.0 (SOP-003 is higher).
