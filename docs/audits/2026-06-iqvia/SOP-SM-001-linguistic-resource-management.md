## 1. Purpose
Defines how Cethos selects, assesses, approves, oversees, and re-evaluates its **linguistic resources** as suppliers — ensuring only qualified resources are used and that their performance is monitored over time. This SOP is the supplier-management layer; the *qualification mechanics* live in **VM-001** (COA translators), **SOP-001** (general translator/reviser qualification) and **SOP-002** (maintenance).

## 2. Scope
Applies to all external linguistic resources, including sub-contracted and agency-supplied staff. Does **not** cover IT/service sub-processors (see **SM-002**) or in-house staff (HR procedures).

## 3. Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Vendor Management Lead | Owns the approved supplier list; runs assessment, approval, and oversight |
| Managing Director | Approves new suppliers for in-scope (e.g. COA) work; approves offboarding |
| Project Manager | Assigns only approved suppliers; reports quality issues |
| Quality Manager | Monthly performance review; periodic spot-checks |

## 4. Definitions
Approved supplier · Linguistic resource · Sub-contractor/agency · Approved Supplier List · Re-evaluation — as used in ISO 17100 §3.1 and the Cethos QMS.

## 5. Supplier selection & assessment
A linguistic resource is assessed against: ISO 17100 §3.1.4 competence basis (documented), §3.1.3 competences (test/quiz), subject-matter competence for the work type (e.g. life-sciences/COA), reference/first-party evidence, NDA + data-handling acknowledgement. For COA work the additional VM-001 §5 criteria apply.

## 6. Approval & the Approved Supplier List
- A resource becomes **approved** only when its qualification is recorded in the QMS (`qms.role_qualifications` + subject-matter where applicable) with documented evidence and a current NDA.
- The **Approved Supplier List** is maintained from the live QMS register (see `approved-supplier-list.md`); COA assignments draw only from the COA-qualified subset.
- Agencies are approved on their ISO 17100 certificate or named linguists' credentials; per-delivery traceability is recorded (`vendor_identifier` / `business_name`).

## 7. Ongoing oversight & re-evaluation
- **Performance monitoring:** monthly review of revision findings, on-time delivery, complaints, and activity (per SOP-002 / §3.1.8).
- **Re-qualification:** every 12 months, or on a serious quality event.
- **Interventions:** coaching → suspension pending remediation → offboarding, all documented.

## 8. Confidentiality & data handling
NDA before any materials are shared; secure transfer only; deletion on completion (mirrors VM-001 §8). Clinical materials follow the client/sponsor confidentiality requirements.

## 9. Non-conformance & corrective action
Quality issues raise a documented non-conformance → root cause → corrective action → follow-up (mirrors VM-001 §9). Records retained ≥5 years.

## 10. SOP review & version control
Reviewed annually or on a process/standard change. Revisions approved by the MD. Prior versions archived.
