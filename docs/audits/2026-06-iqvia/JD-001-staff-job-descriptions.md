> Each role below carries the same baseline training requirement: QMS overview, data protection & confidentiality, information security (SOP-014), and awareness of the SOPs relevant to the role. Clinical-facing roles additionally hold GCP awareness. Competence and training are evidenced per person in FORM-TR-001.

## 1. Founder & CEO — Raminder Shah
**Reports to:** Board / self. **Purpose:** overall strategic direction and accountability for the QMS.
**Key responsibilities:** strategy and business development; client and partner relationships; approval of the Quality Policy and governing documents; management review (SOP-013); resource provision. Currently also holds the **Quality Manager** role on an acting basis with compensating controls (SOP-003).
**Competence:** senior leadership in clinical-research language services; QMS and regulatory familiarity (ISO 17100, GCP, Part 11).

## 2. Managing Director — Amrita Shah
**Reports to:** Founder & CEO. **Purpose:** operational and financial leadership; independent QMS review.
**Key responsibilities:** operational oversight; resource allocation; financial management; coordination across functions; independent review of QMS documents; emergency-recovery delegate (SOP-017-A).
**Competence:** operations and financial management; QMS familiarity.

## 3. Life Sciences Manager — Bobby Rawat
**Reports to:** Founder & CEO. **Purpose:** day-to-day delivery of linguistic services.
**Key responsibilities:** linguistic operations; project coordination; translator assignment and management; quality-control oversight (SOP-010); client communication for linguistic projects.
**Competence:** linguistic-validation / translation operations; therapeutic-area familiarity; GCP awareness.

## 4. Lead Vendor Manager — Ashish Garg
**Reports to:** Founder & CEO / Managing Director. **Purpose:** qualification and oversight of linguistic resources.
**Key responsibilities:** vendor qualification and onboarding (SOP-003); maintenance of the approved supplier list; vendor performance monitoring and re-qualification; sub-processor oversight support (SOP-018).
**Competence:** supplier/vendor management; ISO 17100 §3.1 competence requirements; GCP awareness.

## 5. Project Coordinator, Life Sciences — Karan Verma
