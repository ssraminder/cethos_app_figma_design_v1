## 1. Purpose
The emergency contact and escalation chain for invoking SOP-017. This annex is kept current as contacts change, without re-issuing SOP-017.

## 2. Invocation authority
- **Primary — System Owner:** Raminder Shah (Founder & CEO). Sole authority to declare a disaster and invoke the plan.
- **Emergency-recovery delegate (backup authority):** **Amrita Shah (Managing Director).** May authorise emergency recovery when the System Owner is unavailable — this removes the System-Owner single-point-of-failure noted in SOP-017 §14.

## 3. Escalation / call-tree (in order)
1. **System Owner** (or the delegate, if the System Owner is unreachable) declares the disaster and authorises recovery.
2. **IT Support — Cital Enterprises** executes the technical recovery (PITR restore, storage restore, redeploy).
3. **Platform / provider support** as needed: Supabase support · AWS support · Microsoft 365 support.
4. **Affected clients / sponsors** are notified by the System Owner / Account Manager — honest status + revised timeline.
5. **Acting Quality Manager** opens the incident record and post-incident review (SOP-011 / SOP-013).
