## 3. Step-by-step: chasing a vendor for documents

1. Open the portal → **Vendors** → search the linguist by name or email. *(screenshot)*
2. Open their **vendor detail** page. *(screenshot)*
3. Scroll to **"ISO 17100 evidence — document requests"** → click **Request documents**. *(screenshot)*
4. In the modal, tick the items you need:
   - **Subject specialization evidence** (life-sciences / clinical) — required for all
   - **Professional translation certificate** (e.g. ATA Medical) — if held
   - **Subject specializations / domains** (profile field)
   - For not-yet-qualified linguists, also tick the relevant **competence route** item (degree + experience). *(screenshot)*
5. Paste the COA chase message (see §5) into the **message** box. *(screenshot)*
6. Click **Send request**. The vendor gets a secure upload link (30-day expiry) and automatic reminders. *(screenshot)*

## 4. Step-by-step: recording a qualification (once evidence is in)

1. On the vendor detail page, open the **QMS tab**. *(screenshot)*
2. Review the uploaded evidence (CV, certificate, references). *(screenshot)*
3. Click **Mark vendor qualified** → choose the role(s) and the §3.1.4 basis → save. *(screenshot)*
4. Record the **subject-matter qualification** (Life Sciences / Medical + Clinical Trials (ICF, COA, COG)). *(screenshot)*
5. Confirm the **NDA** is on file (and send one if not). *(screenshot)*
6. Confirm **COA methodology training** completion (once the module is live). *(screenshot)*

## 5. The chase email message (paste into the request)

> Hi {First name} — Cethos is expanding our Clinical Outcome Assessment (COA) translation and linguistic-validation services, and we'd like to include you in our qualified COA linguist panel. To complete your qualification for this work under ISO 17100 and our clinical-quality requirements, please upload the items listed via the secure link. In particular we need documented evidence of your life-sciences / medical specialization — a relevant degree, a medical-translation certification (e.g. ATA Medical), and/or representative clinical or COA work samples. We will contact you separately to arrange a professional reference for your medical/clinical work. Thank you.
